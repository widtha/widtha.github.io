21 Days of Study / 21天研学记

Created2022.08.08|Updated2022.08.11|旅行
|Word count:705|Post View:

At the beginning of July, we conducted an examination and selected some who did well in study to go to this summer camp for the first period. We didn’t need to select them for the second period, and the ending examination was simpler.

19日

中午开学典礼，一位女校长讲话。

At the opening ceremony at noon, and a headmistress spoke.



20日

下午很想家。I feel homesick this afternoon.




21日

下午我们上了“户外扩展”实践课。

In the afternoon, we had a practice class of “outdoor expansion”.

22日

下午拔河，输了，友谊赛赢了。

In the afternoon, we lost the tug of war and won the friendly match.

23日

同桌ZhangJR拿白酒瓶喝水，班主任晚上12点用手电筒晃我们，了解情况，ZhangJR被要求写1000字检讨。

Zhang Jr, my desk mate, drank water from a white wine bottle. The head teacher shook us with a flashlight at 12:00 p.m. to understand the situation. Zhang Jr was asked to write a 1000 word review.

24日

早上5：50就来早读了，后得知不需要今天早读，大部分人去晨练了。

I came to read early [early reading] at 5:50 in the morning and learned that I didn’t need to do this today. Most people went to morning exercise.


25日

晚上和GaoYB做视频，旁边一个用surface pro 8的男数学老师夸GaoYB手表好。

In the evening, I made a video with Gaoyb. A male math teacher who used surface Pro 8 next to me praised Gaoyb’s watch.


26日

下午无人机实践课，先是用电脑模拟，后在户外实机航拍，到我们的时候GaoYB却不来了。

In the afternoon, the UAV practice class, we was first simulated by computer, and then photographed in the outdoor real aircraft. When should we come, Gaoyb did not come.




























27日

晚上班主任^1说要给我们两个买好吃的。

28日

下午做航模实践课。

In the afternoon, I did a model airplane practice class.

29日

食堂大妈怒打一大勺荤菜。

In the afternoon, I did a model airplane practice class.

8月1日

徒步，到欢乐海沙滩吃饭，吃了6个包子。

On foot, we went to the seaside happy sea beach for dinner and ate 6 steamed buns.










2日

下午服装设计实践课。

Afternoon fashion design practice class.


3日

下午第二遍看《风雨哈佛路》。

In the afternoon, I watched Homeless to Harvard: The Liz Murray Story for the second time.

4日

下午玩心理沙盘实践课。

In the afternoon, I played psychological sand table practice class.



5日

中文睡过头了，“逃”了1.5节政治课，他们说这两节课老师就对着课本划了题。晚上考试。

Chinese overslept and “skipped” 1.5 political lessons. They said that the teacher had set a question in the textbook for these two lessons. I have an exam in the evening.

6日

考试。

examination

7日

下午用班里的电脑看了两集柯南，晚上看了一半《死寂》。

In the afternoon, we watched two episodes of Detective Conan on the computer in my class. In the evening, we watched Dead Silence silence.

8日

早上颁奖。10：40坐车回到文华^2门口。

Awards in the morning. 10: 40, we take the bus back to the gate of Wenhua.
