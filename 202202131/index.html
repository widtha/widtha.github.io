About Blog

On February 11, 2022, I initially set up my first blog in four hours. After two days of revision, I wrote my first article.

Thoughts at night / August 22, 2022

This website should not be like this!

I think that writing these is an unfocused and aimless behavior:

  1. Redundancy and no horizontal interface
  2. Content written solely for the purpose of writing articles

So you will see these in the future:

  1. My travel notes
  2. My thoughts, comments, memories and other long articles
  3. More concise interface

I think the significance of this website is to record life, write what you want to express, listen to others’ suggestions, and improve your way of speaking
